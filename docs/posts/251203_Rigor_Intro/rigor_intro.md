The concept of rigor in training and education may be a surprisingly complex and difficult for an organization to get
right. This introduction to the topic focuses on how a specific stated learning goal's rigor and potential for
unnecessary toil can be measured using estimates for total hours to complete the goal and its alignment to Bloom's
Taxonomy of Learning.
