### Welcome
This blog is intended to facilitate understand and discussion of how to evaluate and improving the rigor in vocational
training. The target audience is curriculum developers and organizational leaders new to the academic study of rigor and
how it can be used to improve learning outcomes.

Please feel free to [leave feedback](https://forms.gle/84mCCnyD1gWKGX7B9) including topics you feel would be interesting and appropriate for a public
discussion.
